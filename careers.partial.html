Careers

Rollins Real Estate Management is always looking for Licensed Commercial Real Estate Agents to grow our team.

We provide a welcoming environment for entrepreneur thinkers and strives to actively challenge our agents to further their careers. We are committed to fielding team of the most qualified commercial real estate professionals in the area.

If you strive for excellence and have the strong desire to be a top providing agent, consider joining Rollins Real Estate Management team. Make a commitment to provide your clients with the highest level of commercial real estate services in the area.

Qualifications include:

  • Maryland Real Estate Salesman or Broker License
  • Self-motivated
  • Energetic
  • Enjoys selling
  • Willingness to make Cold Calls
  • Openness to Learning

Some of the benefits of building a career with Rollins Real Estate Management include:

  • Progressive commission split
  • Independence
  • No assigned territories

If you have been considering a career in the field of commercial real estate and you would like to know more information about opportunities with us please email your resume to info@rrem.com or fax to (301) 263-8059.
